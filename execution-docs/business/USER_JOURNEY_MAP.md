Initial journeys:

- Customer browses catalog, views product, starts checkout and tracks order state.
- Admin creates or updates product information.
- Admin reviews orders, payment state, inventory impact and shipping readiness.
- Operational user receives notifications that require action.

Open migration work: confirm journeys with business owners before converting them into page requirements and acceptance scenarios.
